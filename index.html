What the fuss is about

As the 'Doge' crypto names exist, many of us does not realize the real meaning of it. Love for dogs does not really represents in just supporting the name but it needs to put a real purpose kn it. In DogeMoonCash, we will provide grants for the highest quality canine health research and shares information on the discoveries that help prevent, treat and cure canine diseases and rescue stray dogs out of the street and help the find their way home.

DogeMoonCash is inspired by TikTok artists and YouTube vloggers that adopts stray dogs and providing shelters for them. It moves us to make a way to find supporters and real lovers of Dogs. We wanted to provide them the perfect care a pet deserves.
